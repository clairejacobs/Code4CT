Chicken & Mushroom Pasta

Chicken and mushroom pasta are one of the quickest dishes to prepare and is absolutely delicious!!The perparation time for this awesome meal is only 30 minutes!

Ingredients

  • kosher salt
  • 1 lb. corkscrew pasta or cavatappi
  • 1 tsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 lb. Boneless Skinless Chicken Breast
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp. unsalted butter
  • 8 oz. sliced cremini mushrooms
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 3 tbsp. all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 c. half-and-half
  • 1 1/2 c. low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1/2 c. freshly grated Parmesan, plus more for serving
  • 1 tsp. chopped fresh oregano
  • 1 c. baby arugula, roughly chopped

Directions

In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook pasta according to package directions until al dente. Drain, reserving 1/2 cup pasta water, and return to pot. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, heat oil. Season chicken with salt and pepper. Sear chicken until golden and no longer pin about 8 minutes per side. Transfer to a plate to let rest, then cut into 1‚Äù cubes. Meanwhile, return skillet to medium-high heat and melt butter.
